Quick Start

Step One Compile the util lib and server lib 1> c("/wherever/sc.erl"). {ok,sc} 2> c("/wherever/htstub.erl"). {ok,htstub}

Step Two Good to go, try it out 3> htstub:serve(fun(_) -> "

Hello, world from htstub!

" end). <0.2050.0>

Step Three Pull up your web browser and go to 127.0.0.1.
